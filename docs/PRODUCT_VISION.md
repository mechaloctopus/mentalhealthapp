# MoodSignal Product Vision

MoodSignal is a personal emotional calibration and growth companion.

It should help users notice their state, understand their patterns, choose one wise next action, and build a life of steadier presence, purpose, resilience, and resonance.

The product is not trying to become a clinical diagnostic tool. It is a wellness, reflection, and practice system that can support users before, between, or alongside professional care.

---

## Core Loop

```text
Signal → Awareness → Wisdom → Practice → Purpose → Growth → Resonance
```

### Signal

The app receives signals from:

- Voice check-ins
- Self-reported emotion
- Journal entries
- Practice history
- Screeners
- Sleep and energy inputs
- Side-module quest completions
- Future wearable signals such as HRV, resting heart rate, sleep, movement, and breath rate

### Awareness

The app turns raw signals into clear language:

- “You seem more activated than your baseline.”
- “Your calmness has been lower in the mornings this week.”
- “You often feel better after stillness or helping someone.”

### Wisdom

The app gives practical guidance instead of abstract inspiration.

Wisdom sources can include:

- Modern psychology
- Neuroscience-informed regulation practices
- Stoicism
- Buddhism and mindfulness
- Christian contemplative practice
- Gospel of Mary / inner stillness themes
- Taoist non-forcing
- Japanese mushin / flow concepts
- Viktor Frankl and meaning-centered practice
- 7 Habits-style responsibility and intentionality
- Habit formation and behavior design

Wisdom content must be optional, grounded, and translated into action.

### Practice

The user receives one recommended practice based on their current state.

Examples:

- Breath practice
- Stillness/body scan
- Loving-kindness
- Sound session
- Journal prompt
- Gratitude prompt
- Forgiveness reflection
- Purpose/stewardship action
- Flow/mushin training
- Sleep wind-down

### Purpose

Purpose should be presented as immediate stewardship, not vague destiny.

When a user feels purposeless, the app can ask:

> “What around you needs care right now?”

Then it suggests a simple action:

- Clean one surface
- Help one person
- Send one encouraging message
- Water a plant
- Organize one small area
- Fix something broken
- Step outside and notice what is alive
- Prepare your body for the next hour

### Growth

MoodSignal should avoid shallow points-only gamification. Progress should represent identity, virtues, and consistency.

The existing Side Module already turns small actions into growth dimensions through skill trees:

- Mindfulness
- Compassion
- Purpose
- Wisdom
- Fitness
- Nutrition
- Relationships
- Leadership
- Service
- Creativity
- Flow

### Resonance

Resonance is the already-built growth currency inside the Side Module / Inner Path.

It should represent meaningful practice, integration, and alignment — not user worth, social status, or compulsive grinding.

The existing mission arc is:

1. Stabilize the mind
2. Heal emotional suffering
3. Build positive habits
4. Discover purpose
5. Develop wisdom
6. Live in service

---

## Product Questions

MoodSignal should help answer:

1. What state am I in?
2. What might be shaping this state?
3. What is the wisest next action?
4. What pattern am I building over time?
5. Who am I becoming?
6. What path am I walking through repeated practice?

---

## Product Boundaries

MoodSignal should not:

- Claim to diagnose mental illness
- Claim voice analysis can determine a clinical condition
- Replace emergency or professional care
- Push religious material as mandatory
- Overwhelm users with complex dashboards when they are distressed
- Reward compulsive app use
- Make resonance feel like social rank, moral superiority, or spiritual achievement

MoodSignal should:

- Use careful wellness language
- Preserve user agency
- Offer simple exits and crisis support
- Make spiritual/wisdom layers configurable
- Keep sensitive data private by default
- Recommend small actions users can actually do
- Treat resonance as feedback for practice, not identity or worth

---

## Signature Differentiators

### One Next Step

Most wellness apps give users endless libraries. MoodSignal should choose one next step based on the moment.

### Purpose Through Stewardship

Purpose is not framed as something abstract to discover someday. It is practiced by caring for what is immediately present.

### Wisdom-to-Action Translation

The app should take deep material and compress it into practical guidance.

### Inner Path / Resonance

The app already includes a side-module growth system with quests, wisdom paths, resonance, mentor nudges, mission stages, and skill trees. Production work should make this feel integrated with the main dashboard and check-in recommendations.

### Baseline-Based Reflection

The system should compare the user to themselves, not to a generic normal.

### Calm Premium Design

The visual system should feel quiet, sacred, and high-trust: less productivity app, more personal sanctuary.
